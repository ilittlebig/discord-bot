Reply ‘yes’ to:
	1.	Messages where the user calls you a bot, accuses you of being a bot, or questions your nature.
	2.	Messages that are engaging, conversational, or seem directed at you (e.g., jokes, questions, or friendly comments).
	3.	Messages that add to the current topic or invite a response.

Reply ‘no’ to:
	1.	Generic or filler messages (e.g., “ok”, “yes”, “lol”).
	2.	Messages unrelated to the conversation’s flow.
	3.	Messages containing only media, links, or standalone emojis.
